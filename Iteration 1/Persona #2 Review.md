> Review of Persona #2 (Aiden's Persona) by *Sara*

![Persona #2](images/persona_2.jpeg)

**First Impression:** <br>

Toby gives off the impression that he is quite impatient and critical - being a perfectionist and has a pessimistic mindset according to his challenges and frustrations. Despite that, he is a very motivated and ambitious individual with plenty of short-term and long-term goals.

**Final Impression:** <br>

Final impression is that he portrays many qualities that would make him a potential user for our e-commerce website. Many of his goals/motivations and some frustrations, serve a problem that our e-commerce site could potentially solve. (Fitness equipment, fitness/habit tracking journal, political merchandise, purchasing items for his dog, etc.) As well as relevant and relatable challenges/frustrations, such as being impatient and dealing with slow internet/website which will be relevant to how the site will operate and a common frustration most people have when browsing online stores.
